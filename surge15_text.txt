"Most fitness apps follow you. Surge starts from where you already are."

You've got a spot. A park, a sports field, a stretch of beach — somewhere accessible to train. But there is an issue. GPS trackers assume you're going somewhere new. Workout apps ignore the running entirely.

Surge is built for the in-between: the person who wants to run their 500m loop four times, knock out 20 burpees, run it again, and track if they're getting better at the whole thing — not just the run, not just the reps, but the complete session.

Two things. A route. A plan. Nothing left but to Surge.
